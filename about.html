About Us

History: VIIT Alumni Association was registered as an association under Mumbai societies registration act 1950 under the name VIIT Alumni Association, Pune with regn no: F-30342(P) dated 18.9.2007 VIIT Alumni Association pursued a three point objective, namely: Bringing together all the Alumni, Motivating alumni towards the fields of education and culture, and To develop a feeling of oneness and fraternity amongst the alumni of VIIT Aims of the Association: The aim of the association shall be to assist the college and the students in their growth and development so that each passing engineer comes out as a responsible citizen of his/her motherland, aprominent socialite, and above all, a good human being, which the institute, the association and the society is proud of. The association will always work towards the betterment of the alma mater of its members. It shall invest and divert all its resources towards maintaining and enhancing the brand equity of the institute. The association will promote good fellowship among the members of the association and the current students of VIIT. It will encourage and assist the current students of VIIT in maintaining links with the college and each other. The association shall, in participating with the college, pursue these aims by the following ways:

  • Distinguishing the excellence of students in the fields of academics, sports, cultural and social activities.
  • Organizing social, cultural, sporting and technical events
  • Donating infrastructure to the college for assisting students and staff in their research and development related activities as well as imparting knowledge in some technical / non â€“ technical field.
  • Helping the students in their personal development by supporting them through guidance, finance and all other possible assistance.
  • Helping the staff in their quality improvement programme.
